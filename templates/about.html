About Us

Our Mission and Technology

We're dedicated to revolutionizing subjective answer evaluation through advanced AI technologies.

Our Mission

The Subjective Answer Evaluation System was born from a simple observation: evaluating subjective answers is time-consuming, inconsistent, and often biased. Our mission is to create a tool that helps educators provide fair, consistent, and timely feedback to students, while reducing the workload on teachers.

Technical Approach

Our system uses a combination of advanced natural language processing techniques:

  • Semantic Similarity Analysis: We use transformer-based models to understand the meaning behind answers, not just keyword matching.
  • Contextual Understanding: Our system considers the context of the question to evaluate the relevance of the answer.
  • Key Point Detection: We identify essential concepts that should be present in a complete answer.
  • Coherence Evaluation: We assess the logical flow and organization of the response.

System Architecture

Our system is built on a modern, scalable architecture:

  • Frontend: Flask web application with responsive design
  • Backend: Python-based API with optimized NLP processing
  • Models: Fine-tuned transformer models for specific subject domains
  • Database: Secure storage for questions, reference answers, and evaluation history

Accuracy and Validation

Our system has been validated through extensive testing:

  • 90%+ correlation with human graders across multiple subjects
  • Tested on over 10,000 real student answers
  • Continuous improvement through feedback from educators
